Who We Are?

The Tyre Station Weston St. is your local tyre dealer. We are a fully British shop that provides roadworthy tyres to car owners and offer tyre repairs within Westion St. We arrange for our clients the best prices for high quality tyres. Our prices are inclusive of tubeless valves, fitting, balancing and the final disposal of the old tyres..
